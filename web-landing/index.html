How it works

Set up once, in about a minute

1

Install the app

Download the APK and open it. Sign up with email or Google, no card, no catch.

2

Grant two permissions

Turn on Usage Access (screen-time) and Accessibility (reel counting). Everything is read on your device.

3

Scroll like normal

ScrollIQ quietly measures your day and shows your Brain Score, reel count, and where you rank against friends.

Your usage stays on your device

ScrollIQ reads aggregate foreground usage and counts reels locally to compute your Brain Score. It never inspects the content of any app outside the short-video feeds it tracks, and only your score & totals are synced so friends can see the leaderboard.

Installation

How to install the APK

ScrollIQ isn't on Google Play yet, so you install it directly. Takes about a minute.

  1. 1
    Download the app. Tap Download for Android and wait for the .apk to finish downloading.
  2. 2
    Open the file. Open it from your notification shade or your Files → Downloads folder.
  3. 3
    Allow install from this source. If Android warns you, tap Settings, enable Allow from this source for your browser or Files app, then go back.
  4. 4
    Install & open. Tap Install, then Open. Sign up and grant the Usage & Accessibility permissions so ScrollIQ can measure your Brain Score.

The “unknown source” prompt on Android 8+ is per-app, so you're only trusting the app you just downloaded.
